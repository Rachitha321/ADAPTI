Core Technology

Advanced Drift Adaptation

Detecting and reacting to structural changes in network traffic patterns.

DDM (Drift Detection)

The Drift Detection Method monitors the error rate of predictions. Increasing errors trigger Warning or Drift states, signaling that the current model may no longer match the reality of the incoming traffic.

EDDM (Early Detection)

Early Drift Detection Method focuses on the distance between successful classifications. It is particularly sensitive to slow, gradual changes in threat behavior, providing an earlier warning than standard methods.

Real-Time Re-Weighting

Instead of global retraining, our system incrementally re-weights its knowledge, allowing it to "unlearn" outdated patterns while rapidly incorporating new threat signatures into its logic.

Continuous Validation

The system maintains a verification loop to ensure that adaptation improves performance. If a pattern shifts back, the dynamic model can re-adjust to previously learned normal states.
